
##### Rules
----

1. Power rule: if $x^n$ then $\int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}+C$ where $(n \neq -1)$
2. Exponential functions: $\int a^x dx = \frac{a^x}{ln(a)} + C$
3. Logarithm: $\int \frac{1}{x}dx = ln|x| +C$
4. Trigonometric basics: $\int cos(x)dx = sin(x) + C$ and $\int sin(x)dx = -cos(x)+C$
5. Linearity: $\int(f(x) + g(x))dx = \int f(x)dx + \int g(x) dx$

##### Other tools
----

1. **Theory of variable substitution**: when part of a function is inside another function. Example:
$$\int 2x cos(x^2)dx$$
Let u = x^2 -> works perfectly

2. **Integration by parts** used when there's a product of two functions. Example
$$\int xe^x dx$$

and more.. (in more advanced cases)
